Eligibility Requirements

This grant provides support towards the costs of the purchase and installation of EV charging points at residential properties when also installing a cross-pavement charging solution.

You can’t register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.

To be eligible for this grant you must meet all the following criteria:

  • adequate on-street parking
    • street parking must be generally available outside or near your home
    • the on-street parking is legal and does not disrupt the safety or flow of vehicles and pedestrians
  • vehicle requirements
    • you must be the registered keeper of an eligible electric vehicle
  • property requirements
    • your property must be in England, Wales, Scotland or Northern Ireland
    • if your property is in the Channel Islands or the Isle of Man, you will not be considered eligible.
